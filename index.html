About
Delete bad memories on Facebook

This will pernamently delete the selected data. If these are what others posted / tagged you in, hide them from your Timeline.

Absolutely none of your data is sent to me or anywhere other than Facebook's servers. It uses Chronimum, it's a robot Chrome browser working on your computer.

Be sure to get a backup of your FB data from here: https://www.facebook.com/help/1701730696756992, just in case you accidently delete your important photos.

Use this app at your own risk.
